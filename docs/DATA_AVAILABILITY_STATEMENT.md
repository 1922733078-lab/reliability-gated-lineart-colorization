# Data Availability Statement

The raw training and validation images used in the associated study are not redistributed in this repository. They may be subject to licensing, copyright, and redistribution constraints.

Suggested manuscript wording:

> The raw image data used in this study are not publicly redistributed because of licensing and redistribution constraints. The public repository releases the method code, configuration templates, fuzzy rules, and selector/gate implementations needed to inspect the reported decision pipeline. Additional access conditions can be discussed with the corresponding author where appropriate.

This repository excludes:

- raw training and validation images
- trained checkpoints and model weights
- generated qualitative outputs
- private logs and local workspace files
- third-party helper-tool copies with uncertain redistribution scope
